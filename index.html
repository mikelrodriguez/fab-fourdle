How to Play

Guess the hidden Beatles lyric at the bottom by guessing valid 5-letter words in the top grid.

For every valid 5-letter word you guess:

  • If the letter exists in the target phrase, it will turn Green and be revealed below.
  • If the letter does not exist in the phrase, it will turn Gray.

You have 5 attempts to reveal the required letters to win the game!
